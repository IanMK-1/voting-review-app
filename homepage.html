VOTING AND REVIEW APPLICATION

About

The voting-Review Application, is an application used for electrol purposes and aslo to rate and review the leaders that have been elected. The application helps in reducing the electrol process time, which is viewed of being a tidious process. The application aims to make the electrol process in Kenya transparent, accountable and easy.

  • Whatever voting method is used, the system is simple, accurate, verifiable, secure, accountable and transparent;
  • The votes cast are counted, tabulated and the results are clear;
